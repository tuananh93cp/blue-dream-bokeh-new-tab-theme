PRIVACY POLICY - Blue Dream Bokeh New Tab Theme
===============================================

Last Updated: March 7, 2025

OVERVIEW
--------
Blue Dream Bokeh New Tab Theme ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome/Firefox extension.

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT
------------------------------
• Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information including names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

• Browsing Data: We do not track, monitor, or collect your browsing history, search queries, or website visits.

• User Activity: We do not monitor or record your activities within the extension or on any websites.

• Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information.

• Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, operating system, or browser.

WHAT THE EXTENSION DOES
-----------------------
• Replaces your new tab page with a beautiful blue bokeh background
• Displays current time on the new tab page
• Provides a search box that redirects to Google search (standard web search)
• Stores minimal local preferences using Chrome's storage API (only for theme settings)

LOCAL STORAGE
-------------
The extension may use Chrome's local storage API to save your preferences (such as theme settings). This information:
• Is stored locally on your device only
• Is never transmitted to external servers
• Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
--------------------
• Search functionality redirects to Google.com - Google's privacy policy applies to search queries
• No other third-party services are used

DATA SHARING
------------
We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties because we do not collect any information.

PERMISSIONS USED
----------------
• "storage": Used only to save your local theme preferences
• "chrome_url_overrides": Used to replace the new tab page with our custom design
